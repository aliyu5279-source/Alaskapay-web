## Production Notes

### Camera/Screen Recording
- Use iOS Simulator or actual device with screen recording
- Ensure all animations are smooth (60fps recording)
- Use realistic data (no Lorem Ipsum)
- Show actual biometric authentication animation
- Include haptic feedback visual cues

### Editing Requirements
- Color grade for vibrant, professional look
- Add subtle motion blur for transitions
- Include UI sound effects (taps, success sounds)
- Sync voiceover perfectly with visuals
- Add subtle background music (royalty-free)

### Text Overlays
- Font: SF Pro Display (iOS native) or similar
- Size: Large, readable on mobile
- Color: White with subtle shadow or dark with white background
- Animation: Fade in/out, never jarring
- Duration: 2-3 seconds each

## Technical Specifications for App Store

### Video Requirements
- **Aspect Ratio**: 9:16 (Portrait)
- **Resolution**: 1080x1920 or 886x1920
- **Supported Devices**: iPhone 15 Pro Max, iPhone 14 Pro Max, iPhone 13 Pro Max
- **File Format**: .mp4 or .mov
- **Codec**: H.264 or HEVC
- **Max File Size**: 500MB
- **Max Duration**: 30 seconds

### Audio Requirements
- **Format**: AAC or MP3
- **Sample Rate**: 44.1 kHz or 48 kHz
- **Bit Rate**: 128 kbps minimum
- **Channels**: Stereo
- **Volume**: Normalized, no clipping

## App Store Upload Instructions

1. Log into App Store Connect
2. Navigate to your app
3. Go to "App Preview and Screenshots"
4. Select device size (iPhone 15 Pro Max)
5. Upload video file
6. Add preview poster frame
7. Test playback on actual device
8. Submit for review

## Success Metrics

After video goes live, track:
- **View Rate**: % of users who watch video
- **Completion Rate**: % who watch full 30 seconds
- **Conversion Rate**: Downloads after viewing
- **A/B Test**: With/without video performance
- **Engagement**: Time spent on App Store page

Target: 60%+ completion rate, 20%+ conversion lift
