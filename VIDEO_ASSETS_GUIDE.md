## üöÄ Upload Instructions

### App Store Connect
1. Log in to appstoreconnect.apple.com
2. Select "Alaska Pay" app
3. Go to version you're preparing
4. Click "App Preview and Screenshots"
5. Select "iPhone 15 Pro Max" (6.7" display)
6. Click "+" to add preview
7. Upload video file
8. Select poster frame (best looking moment)
9. Preview on device
10. Save and submit

### Poster Frame Recommendations
- **Best**: Dashboard with balance (Frame 2)
- **Alternative**: Send money success (Frame 3)
- **Backup**: Virtual cards display (Frame 4)
