## App Preview Video Guidelines

### Technical Specifications
- Duration: 15-30 seconds (App Store), up to 2 minutes (Play Store)
- Format: MP4 (H.264), MOV
- Resolution: 1080p minimum, 4K recommended
- Frame rate: 30 fps minimum
- Audio: Optional but recommended
- File size: Under 500MB

### Content Guidelines
- Show actual app functionality
- No marketing fluff or stock footage
- Clear, readable text overlays
- Smooth transitions
- Professional voiceover or captions
- Brand consistent colors and fonts
- See APP_PREVIEW_VIDEO_STORYBOARD.md for detailed script

## Age Rating

### App Store
**Rating: 4+** (Ages 4 and up)
- No objectionable content
- Financial app suitable for all ages with parental guidance

### Play Store
**Rating: Everyone**
- ESRB: Everyone
- No mature content
- Financial literacy appropriate for supervised use

## Content Rating Questionnaire Answers

**Violence:** None
**Sexual Content:** None
**Profanity:** None
**Drugs/Alcohol:** None
**Gambling:** None (real money transactions are financial, not gambling)
**User-Generated Content:** Yes (profile pictures, support messages)
**User Communication:** Yes (support chat)
**Personal Information Collection:** Yes (required for financial services)
**Location Sharing:** Optional (for fraud prevention)
